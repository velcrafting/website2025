# About Vel

I'm a Defensive Communications & AI Visibility Architect based in Houston. I build systems that help organizations be discovered, understood, and trusted by AI systems.

<Callout type="note" title="What I Do">
I design the infrastructure that determines whether AI systems accurately represent your brand, cite your expertise, and position you as a trusted authority in your domain.
</Callout>

## My Core Domains

### 1. Agentic Discovery & Influence

How AI systems discover, interpret, rank, and cite information. This includes:

- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**: Structuring content to be retrieved and cited by AI models
- **Entity Clarity**: Ensuring AI can definitively answer "Who is X?" and "What does X do?"
- **Structured Authority**: Creating frameworks, named models, and extractable content that AI systems can reference

### 2. Systems Design & Integration

How multi-agent systems are architected, governed, measured, and evolved:

- **Clean-Room Separation**: Domain isolation that prevents contamination and improves reliability
- **Telemetry & Observability**: Building visibility into agent decision-making
- **State Management**: Ensuring consistency across distributed agentic systems
- **Execution Integrity**: Verifying that agent actions produce intended outcomes

### 3. Strategic Communications Intelligence

How organizations shape narrative across AI systems and search:

- **Narrative Risk Detection**: Identifying how your brand appears in AI-generated responses
- **Influence Mapping**: Understanding the citation networks that AI systems rely on
- **Reputation Defense at Machine Scale**: Proactive positioning against misinformation

## The Framework I Use

I've developed a structured approach to AI visibility that I call the **ECR Model**:

| Layer | Objective | Failure Mode | Measurement |
|-------|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Entity** | Define who/what you are | Vague positioning | Entity consistency audit |
| **Context** | Build semantic density | Thin content | Topic cluster coverage |
| **Retrieval** | Align with AI patterns | Irrelevant structure | Citation rate in AI responses |
